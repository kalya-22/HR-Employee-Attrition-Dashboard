# HR Employee Attrition Dashboard using Power BI

## Project Overview

This project presents an interactive HR Employee Attrition Dashboard developed using Power BI to analyze employee turnover patterns and identify factors contributing to attrition.

## Objectives

* Analyze employee attrition trends.
* Identify departments with higher employee turnover.
* Study the relationship between overtime and attrition.
* Explore attrition across different age groups and job roles.

## Dashboard Features

* KPI Cards:

  * Total Employees
  * Employees Left
  * Attrition Rate
  * Average Monthly Income

* Interactive Filters:

  * Department
  * Gender
  * Age Group
  * Job Role

* Visualizations:

  * Employee Attrition by Department
  * Employee Attrition by Age Group
  * Overtime Distribution
  * Employee Attrition by Job Role

## Key Insights

* Overall employee attrition rate is 16.1%.
* Research & Development department experiences the highest employee attrition.
* Employees working overtime show higher attrition tendencies.
* Employees in the 26-35 age group form a significant portion of the workforce.

## Tools Used

* Power BI Desktop
* Microsoft Excel
* HR Employee Attrition Dataset
